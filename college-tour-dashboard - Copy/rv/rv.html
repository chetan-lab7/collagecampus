About RV University

Established under the RV University Act, RV University (RVU) is a leading private institution in Bangalore, Karnataka. The university focuses on interdisciplinary learning and research, offering world-class education through its various schools. The campus is located in the heart of Bangalore, providing state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant academic environment.
