About CMR University

Established under the CMR University Act of 2013, CMR University is a prestigious private institution in Bangalore, Karnataka. The university's Lakeside Campus spans 60 acres of lakefront property off Hennur-Bagalur Main Road in Chagalatti, designed to provide an enriching learning environment.
